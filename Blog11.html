WHY SHOULD WE THINK POSITIVELY?

By Vanshika Shah

July 6, 2020

Building yourself a new hope all by yourself is an enchanting feeling.


Connecting to certain people and bringing some relations to fruition is happiness in itself. We all build up beams of faith for all the things we want, we laugh, we cry, we laugh till we cry.


Change is quintessential and we find things about ourselves that we never knew we could be made of or could go through and come out of, invincible and stronger than ever.


We have our highs and lows, we get lost, we get pulled down but we shall never let the weight of the world make us weak.


To all the souls who feel they would never get off their sorrows,


Believe in yourself and in your morals


Be postive even when things seem gloomy


People will judge you no matter what, so do whatever makes your heart smile


Be kind to people for no reason


And most importantly,


Have faith that you are beautiful, flawed but only human..


Trust me, the process of finding yourself is subtle but the most beautiful experience ever.
