MAIN

By Vinit Vora

July 23, 2020

    Main
    Paida hote hi, janab Khushi se bole ladka hua h.
    Maa hasi se roo rahi thi aur, main na jane kyu shor macha raha tha.
    Papa ne hath me uthaya, maa ne gale se lagya par yar chote bhai ki ungli bhen ne hi thami,
    Sabhi fule nai sama rahe the aur main, main bas tabhi sun tha.

    Jab chalna seekha tab dadu ki lakdi bana,
    Jab daud na seekha tab unki dawai bana,
    Jab hasa na seekha tab unke assu bana,
    Jab samj na seekha tab, tab main unka sahara bana.

    Kuch alag tha who jab naye log bane,
    Kuch apne bane aur kuch milte hi paraye ho gaye,
    Khass the who jo, jinhone sath nibhaya,
    Pass rehte hanesha mere aur main, main bas khush tha.

    Zindagi h ek paheli dosto
    Yeh h ek rangeen sapna, sunhere h iske sabhi rang
    Apne h iske saye, jiske saye me main rang purr raha hu
    Who ajj sab sath h aur main, main bas khush hu?!
